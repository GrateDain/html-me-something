Stop One

     After flying into San Jose we drove to the beach community of XXX where we checked into our forrest bungalo and got settled in. The next day we started early with a walk down to a local chocolate farm and cafe for breakfast before touring (AND TASTING!) the operation from tree to shiny wrapped bar. Our favorite part was easily the end, where high on a cliff with Caribean breezes we made our own chocolate mixes using various spices and herbs. Garlic, chili and sea salt chocolate, delicious who knew!
Having spent the morning trekking up and down the vertical trails of the chocolate farm, we took the afternoon easy, picking up supplies then relaxing at the beach. Watched surfers, swam and just layed about til a sunset stroll home. We closed our first full day at the restraunt of our host, who prior to setteling down in Costa Rica was an chef on yachts across the world. An amazing buffet feast of Thai, Indian and Sushi; along with a local favorites didn't disappoint. He even joined us for a glass of his special house made chocolate infused liqour, which was the perfect nutty rich compliment to our shared slice of chocolate cake.
Sated, stuffed and blissful, we explored the night markets a bit on our way home to a sound slumber.
